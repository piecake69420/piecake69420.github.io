Examiners report

Most candidates achieved some marks for this question, with many achieving full marks. A common error was only to describe the illustrated age-gender pyramid and not suggest how it may change in the next 30 years. Many recognised that over the years it would become an aging population with a decrease in birth rate. Few students recognised that Brazil is currently at stage 3 and moving to stage 4 of the demographic transition model or that the growth rate would decrease, although the overall population would initially continue to rise.
