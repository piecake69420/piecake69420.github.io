Question

Define the term pollution.

Markscheme

substances or energy produced by human activity that has a detrimental/negative effect on organisms and/or environments;
The addition to an environment of a substance or an agent (such as heat) by human activity, at a rate greater than that at which it can be rendered harmless by the environment, and which has an appreciable effect on the organisms within it; OWTTE

 

[1 max]

Examiners report

Few definitions were complete, many were too vague and lacked reference to ‘substances or energy produced by human activity’.
