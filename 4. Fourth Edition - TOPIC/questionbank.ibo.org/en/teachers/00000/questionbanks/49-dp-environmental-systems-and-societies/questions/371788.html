Examiners report

Most candidates correctly identified sunlight as the source of energy. A common mistake was to state producers/plants/phytoplankton.

a.i.

Most candidates answered this question well. Common answers included ‚Äòheat loss from respiration‚Äô or ‚Äòmigration of kingfishers/birds‚Äô. Some answers were considered too vague such as just ‚Äòrespiration‚Äô or ‚Äòdeath of species‚Äô.

a.ii.

The majority of candidates were able to draw a food chain correctly. Common errors included incorrect direction for the flow of energy/arrows or drawings of foodwebs or pyramid of numbers.

a.iii.

This question was generally well answered. In some cases answers were too vague e.g. ‚Äòchanges would occur in food chain/some species would increase/decrease‚Äô or ‚Äòchange in biodiversity‚Äô.

a.iv.
